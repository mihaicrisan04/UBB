# Numerical Calculus Exam, 8

1) Let $f \in C^3[0, 1]$.

   a) (2 points) Find $(Pf)(x)$, the polynomial interpolating the function $f$ at the double node $x_0 = 0$ and the simple node $x_1 = 1$.

   b) (1 point) Integrate $\displaystyle\int_{0}^{1}(Pf)(x)\,dx$ to obtain a quadrature formula for $\displaystyle\int_{0}^{1}f(x)\,dx$. Find its degree of precision.

   c) (1.5 points) Use Peano's theorem to find the remainder $R(f)$ of the quadrature formula in b).

2) (2.5 points) Find the Cholesky decomposition of the Hilbert matrix

$$H_3 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1/2 & 1/3 \\ 1/2 & 1/3 & 1/4 \\ 1/3 & 1/4 & 1/5 \end{bmatrix}.$$

3) (2 points) Show that the polynomial $3x^4 - 8x^3 + 6x^2 - x$ has $\alpha = 1$ as a root. Does the iteration $x_{n+1} = 3x_n^4 - 8x_n^3 + 6x_n^2$ converge to $\alpha$ for a suitable $x_0$? Study the convergence of the method, find the order of convergence and give a bound for the error.
