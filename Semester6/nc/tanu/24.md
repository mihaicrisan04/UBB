# Numerical Calculus Exam, 24

1) (3 points) Use Cholesky factorization to solve the system

$$\begin{cases} 4x_1 + 2x_2 + 6x_3 = -2 \\ 2x_1 + 2x_2 + 5x_3 = 0 \\ 6x_1 + 5x_2 + 22x_3 = 1 \end{cases}$$

2) (2 points) Define a one-point iterative method to approximate the root $\sqrt{5}$, for a sufficiently close $x_0$. Find the order of convergence and a bound for the error of the approximation.

3) Let $f \in C^3[0, 1]$.

   a) (1 point) Find $(Pf)(x)$, the polynomial interpolating the function $f$, knowing $f(0)$, $f(1)$ and $f'(0)$.

   b) (1 point) Integrate $\displaystyle\int_0^1 (Pf)(x)\,dx$ to obtain a quadrature formula for $\displaystyle\int_0^1 f(x)\,dx$. Find its degree of precision.

   c) (1 point) Find $A_0$, $A_1$ and $A_2$ such that the quadrature formula

   $$\int_0^1 f(x)\,dx = A_0 f(0) + A_1 f'(0) + A_2 f(1) + R(f)$$

   has the maximum degree of precision possible. Compare the formula so obtained to the one in part b).

   d) Find the remainder $R(f)$.
