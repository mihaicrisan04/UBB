# Numerical Calculus Exam, 17

> *Handwritten exam in blue ink.*

1) Consider the iteration $x_{n+1} = 2 - (1+c)x_n + cx_n^2$.
$\alpha = 1$, order of convergence, error bound.

2) $f: [-1, 1] \to \mathbb{R}$ twice continuously differentiable. Derive a 1-point numerical integration formula:

$$\int_{-1}^{1} |x|\,f(x)\,dx = A\,f(\alpha) + R(f)$$

with max degree of exactness possible. Express the remainder $R(f)$ in terms of $f$.

3) $A = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 1 \\ 1 & 2 \end{bmatrix}$, $b = \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ -2 \end{bmatrix}$, $Ax = b$. Gauss-Seidel, $x^{(0)} = [0\ 0]^T$.

4) Consider the data $(0, 1)$, $(1, -3)$, $(2, 7)$. Find the linear polynomial.
