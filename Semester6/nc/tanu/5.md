# Numerical Calculus Exam, 5

1) (3 points) Solve the system

$$\begin{cases} -x_1 + 3x_2 + 2x_3 = -1 \\ 2x_1 + 4x_2 \hphantom{+ 0x_3} = 8 \\ x_1 + 2x_2 - 3x_3 = 7 \end{cases}$$

using LUP decomposition with partial pivoting.

2) (2 points) Find the values of $c \in \mathbb{R}$ for which the iterative method $x_{n+1} = 2 - (1+c)x_n + cx_n^2$ converges to $\alpha = 1$ for a suitable $x_0$. Discuss the convergence of the method, the order of convergence and give a bound for the error.

3) Let $f \in C^2[-1, 1]$.

   a) (1.5 points) Find $(Pf)(x)$, the polynomial interpolating the function $f$, given $f(-1)$ and $f'(1)$.

   b) (1 point) Integrate $\displaystyle\int_{-1}^{1}(Pf)(x)\,dx$ to obtain a quadrature formula for $\displaystyle\int_{-1}^{1}f(x)\,dx$. Find its degree of precision.

   c) (1.5 points) Find the remainder $R(f)$ of the quadrature formula in b), using Peano's theorem.
